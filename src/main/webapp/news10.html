上海青少年科学社青少年国际交流项目系列推介会

SSAYT Held International Programs Introduction Meeting

为推动国际化交流、促进科学教育发展、提升青少年科学素质、加强创新人才培育，上海青少年科学社根据当今科技发展的趋势，立足青少年对科技创新的多元化需求，运用社会化工作的新机制，通过“科学种子”、“科学伙伴”、“科学园丁”三项计划的“十五大举措”，搭建科学教育工作和创新人才培育互相融合的社会化平台。其中，5月份的科学种子选拔赛即将启动，采取网上申报、专家评审的方式在全市范围内选拔科学种子人才，培育成为青少年科学社研究会员及高级会员，推荐参加各类国际青少年科技交流活动。

In order to promote international communications, advocate science education, enhance youth scientific qualifications and strengthen the cultivation of innovative talents, SSAYT builds a social platform combining science education work and cultivation of innovative talents based on the youth’s diversified demands for technological innovation, by means of utilizing technological trends and the new mechanism of socialization work, through the “15 measures” including the 3 projects named “science seed”, “science mate” and “science gardener” respectively. The science seed selection in May is about to come, which adopts the ways of online applying and expert reviewing so as to select science talents in the whole city, who would then be fostered to be SSAYT’s research members or high-level members and be recommended to participate in various international youth technology communication activities.



为帮助青少年学生提高国际交流活动参与度，开阔并丰富其眼界视角、增加其科技创新意识、提高其创新能力，2014年上海青少年科学社与各国赛事主办方、非营利组织、科研院所、科技协会等相关机构联系，建立了长期友好合作意向，并将于近日开展青少年国际交流项目系列推介会，集中介绍美国、欧洲、澳大利亚、加拿大、以及亚洲地区等国家各类青少年赛事及交流项目。

In order to assist teenage students to enhance their participation of international affairs, broaden their horizon, increase their scientific awareness and innovation skills, SSAYT managed to get in touch with different competition organizers, non-profit organizations, institutes of scientific research and science and technology associations and established long-term collaborations. Meanwhile, a youth international communication program promotion conference will come up soon, which is aimed to introduce a variety of youth contests and communication programs from the United States, Europe, Australia, Canada and Asia, etc.



2014年青少年国际交流项目推介会主要面向初、高中学生，将统一介绍科学社现有及即将参与的比赛类、展示类、以及游学类项目。推介会上对大赛的背景、参与条件、参与方式等问题作出详细的介绍以及说明。望广大家长、师生持续关注青少年科学社微信，届时积极报名参与。

The 2014 youth international communication program promotion conference is mainly faced to junior and senior high school students. SSAYT’s current and upcoming competition, presentation and tour program will be introduced then. The promotion conference will give very detailed information to the background, enrolling requirement and methods, etc. Dear parents, teachers and students, kindly keep following the SSAYT Wechat account and actively participate in then.
