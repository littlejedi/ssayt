INTERNATIONAL SUMMER CAMPS

Shanghai STEM center will offer several international summer camps for SSAYTs member students in 2015, such as 2015 STEM Cloud Center——International Game Programming Science Explore Summer Camp and x-lab International Science Camp in university of Gottingen.

International Game Programming Science Explore Summer Camp

International Game Programming Science Explore Summer Camp is one of the golden projects provided by Shanghai STEM Cloud Center, aiming at develop science, technology, engineering and mathematics innovation ability for students through STEM education and science exploration.

X-lab International Science Camp in University of Gottingen

X-lab International Science Camp in university of Gottingen is another golden project provided by Shanghai STEM Cloud Center.
