Australian National Chemistry Quiz( ANCQ)

The Australian National Chemistry Quiz is a unique chemical education activity. It provides a major focus for secondary school students on the relevance of chemistry in an exciting and stimulating way. On behalf of the Royal Australian Chemical Institute (RACI), I cordially invite all students at your school to participate in the 2013 Australian National Chemistry Quiz. With over 1, 600 schools entering over 110, 000 students in 2012, I recommend that you consider students from your school for this prestigious competition.

Now SSAYT is in charge of ANCQ in the following areas:

Including Shanghai, Zhejiang, Jiangsu, Anhui, Hubei, Chongqing, Sichuan, Yunnan, Guizhou, Hunan, Jiangxi, Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Hainan provinces and Tibet Autonomous Region
