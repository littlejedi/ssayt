第16届亚洲物理学奥林匹克竞赛在杭州举行

16th APhO took place in Hangzhou



第16届亚洲物理学奥林匹克竞赛于5月4日上午在浙江省杭州市浙江大学内开幕。来自25个国家和地区的26个参赛队共186名中学生参加本届比赛。全国政协副主席、中国科协主席韩启德、浙江省政协副主席、浙江省科协主席姚克、浙江大学校长吴朝晖等领导出席开幕式。

The 16th Asian Physics Olympiad contest has been opened in the University of Zhejiang, which in the Hangzhou, Zhejiang Province. There are totally 186 students take part in this contest, who are come from 25 different countries and 26 teams. There are also some of the responsor take part in this opening ceremony, including Han Qide,CPPCC Vice Chairman, Chairman of China Association; Yao Ke,Vice Chairman of Zhejiang Province;Wu Zhaohui, CAST president, Zhejiang University, Zhejiang University president.

亚洲物理学奥林匹克竞赛是物理学领域重要的国际赛事，源于国际物理奥林匹克竞赛，每年举行一次，是目前亚洲最为重要的物理竞赛。该项赛事旨在通过组织国际性中学生物理竞赛，宣传物理学科的重要性，推动亚洲各个国家和地区在物理学教育方面的交流与合作，激励有才华的青少年脱颖而出。

The 16th Asian Physics Olympiad contest is an important international contest in the region of physics which belong to the International Physics Olympiad Contest, and become one of the most important physics contest in Asia.This contest was established in order to disseminate the importance of Physics subject, boost the communication and cooperation of Physics subjects among the countries of Asia that make the teenagers who get intelligence on physics was discovered.



今年，是中国第二次作为主办国举办该项赛事，由浙江大学、中国科协青少年科技中心和浙江省科协联合承办。据悉，本届比赛，分理论考试和实验考试两场进行，每一场都将持续5个小时，这对参赛选手的知识素养和身体素养都将是一次极大的考验。据出题方浙江大学物理学系介绍，该项竞赛的理论考试，是让考生围绕一个较为前沿的物理主题，环环相扣，不断深化，回答连续的追问；实验考试，则是要考生利用举办方提供的器材自行搭建起实验装置，按要求进行创新性的实验，并通过数据分析，得出明确结论。

This year is the second time for China to be the host of the competition which is held by the University of Zhejiang, the Center of Teenager Science and Technology of Science Association of China and the Science Association of Zhejiang. It is reported that this competition is divided into three separately parts, each of which will last as long as five hours. Undoubtedly, that will be a great challenge on both the capacity of knowledge and the physical quality of the competitors. According to the Physics Faculty of Zhejiang, which is in charge of making the test, the competitors are about to answer gradually deepening questions around a physics theme at the leading edge in the theoretic testing part, while the experimental testing part required them to establish experimental devices independently and proceed an innovative experiment and finally obtain the conclusion through data analysis.

与其他赛事不同，亚洲物理学奥林匹克竞赛奖项的产生也颇具特色。大赛设金、银、铜、荣誉奖及单项奖若干，每个奖项的获奖人数则是根据当届参赛选手的成绩经过复杂的计算而得出，凸显了科学的严谨特点。

Unlike many other different competitions, the awards setting of the Asian Physics Olympiad contest are extraordinary special.There are many kinds of awards in this competition, including gold, silver, cupper and so on. The number of people who were given the prize was calculated by a complex process that prominent the rigor of the science.



今年的比赛不仅有来自亚洲各个国家和地区代表队参赛，还吸引了俄罗斯、罗马尼亚以及澳大利亚等欧洲和大洋洲国家代表队参赛。

This year, the contest not only had teams representing different Asian countries and regions, but also attracted national teams from Europe and Oceania, such as Russia, Romania and Australia.

开幕式上，全国政协副主席、中国科协主席韩启德高度肯定了亚洲物理学奥林匹克竞赛的作用和意义。他说，该赛事的设立体现了亚洲各个国家和地区对物理学后备人才培养的重视，给喜爱物理学科优秀学生以激励和动力，同时也为亚洲优秀年轻人的交流，了解亚洲不同地区的文化提供了非常好的机会。

During the opening ceremony, Qide Han, CPPCC Vice Chairman and the Chairman of China Association, highly affirmed the role and significance of the Asian Physics Olympiad. He said that the establishment of the event reflects that various countries and regions in Asia highly value the physics training of reserve talents, and provide stimulation and motivation to those outstanding students that love physics; this contest also provided a good opportunity for Asian outstanding young people to communicate and learn about different cultures in different regions.
