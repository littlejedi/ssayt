2015年青少年高校科学营上海科学营圆满结束！

2015 Youth College Science Camp —— Shanghai Science Camp Came to a Successful Conclusion!

求索，瞭望
Quest，Outlook
济中华，铸情怀
For China, For Aspiration
梦起科学，扬帆深海
Dream of Science, Sail for Future
-----2015年上海科学营寄语
-----Note of 2015 Shanghai Science Camp


2015年7月19日—25日，来自北京、天津、上海、山东、福建、云南、四川、西藏等26个省、市、自治区和港澳台地区的1145名师生带着科学梦想相聚上海，走进复旦大学、上海交通大学、同济大学、华东师范大学、华东理工大学五所高校度过了一个难忘的七天科技之旅。各地区优秀高中生大展风采，各民族同胞团结友爱，在科学殿堂“放飞科学梦想，共享科技盛宴”——2015年青少年高校科学营上海科学营圆满结束。

From July 19 to July 25, 2015, 1145 teachers and students from 26 provinces, such as Shandong, Fujian, Yunnan, Sichuan; from municipalities, such as Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai; from autonomous regions, such as Tibet; and from HongKong, Macao and Taiwan gathered in Shanghai with the dream of science. They spent their unforgettable seven days on the tech tour to Fudan University, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Tongji University, East China Normal University and East China University of Science and Technology, and senior high school talents from place to place shone there. Most important of all, all ethnic groups showed solidarity and friendship, and flew their dream of science and enjoyed the fruit of tech in the world of science.——2015 Youth College Science Camp —— Shanghai Science Camp came to a successful conclusion.

伴随着7月19日的第一缕曙光，2015年上海科学营迎来了来自祖国大江南北、两岸四地的千余名师生，开启了“上海七天科学之旅”，一场多元化科技文化交流的盛宴正式拉开帷幕。夏令的上海，用她别样的热情欢迎着各民族师生，期待在未来的七天中引领他们在高校科学营中学习科技新知、分享科创成果。

On July 19, 2015 when the first light was seen, more than a thousand of teachers and students from across the country gathered in Shanghai Science Camp. Seven days` adventures of science, which was also a banquet of multicultural communication for science and technology kicked off in Shanghai. In the summertime, the students and teachers of all ethnic groups were welcomed by particular zest of Shanghai, and they were expected to learn new knowledge about science and technology and enjoy innovations in the next seven days.

各地区营员齐聚上海
Campers from Place to Place Gathered in Shanghai


开营式精彩活动
Activities in the Opening Ceremony of Camp


2015年上海科学营充分发挥高校和企业的品牌特色与学科优势，打造以物质科学、生命科学、技术与设计、地球与空间、社会科学五大学科组为分类的科研学习团队。科学营期间开放数十处国家级重点实验室及科研机构，并积极创造条件让营员能够亲自动手参与科研实验，体验国家新型科研器材和成果，使营员们通过走进高校科研场所，亲身体验科技新事物的强大动力；通过走进社会人文场馆，感受城市精神和地域文化特色，领悟上海的沧桑历史和人文情怀。

In 2015, by giving full play to brand characteristics and the advantage of subject of universities and enterprises, Shanghai Science Camp built a team marked by learning and scientific research, which could be classified into five subjects, i.e. physical sciences, bio-science, technology and design, earth and space, social sciences. During the camp, dozens of state key labs and scientific research institutions were accessible and conditions were created for the campers who could do scientific experiments themselves, and experience the new equipment for scientific research and scientific payoffs. All these ensured campers could have firsthand experience of the strong driving force from innovations by entering scientific research places in the colleges and universities; and urban spirit and regional culture by entering humanities buildings. At last, historical vicissitude and humanistic feelings of Shanghai could also be comprehended.

科研场馆行
A Tour in Scientific-research Museum


本次科学营，营员们除了通过实践接触科学，更能通过精彩的讲座聆听科技大师奉献科研的伟大历程，领略大师风采、感悟科学魅力。在短短的七天时间里，2015年上海科学营在复旦分营、交大分营、同济分营、华师大分营、华理工分营、船舶专题营的活动中多次组织营员参加大师讲坛、人文交流等活动，其中包括邀请多位中国科学院院士、各领域的国家级教学名师、享誉国内外的著名科学家以及奋战在科研一线的领军人物为营员们讲解科学的奥秘与科学研究的方法。

In this science camp, the campers could learn about not only science through practice, but also the great course in which masters dedicated themselves to science research by having the superb lectures. During the lectures, they could also catch a glimpse of the masters' elegant demeanour and appreciate the charm of science. Only in these seven days, 2015 Shanghai Science Camp organized campers to participate in master class and cultural and educational exchanges in the activities of Fudan University sub-camp, Shanghai Jiao Tong University sub-camp, Tongji University sub-camp, East China Normal University sub-camp, East China University of Science and Technology sub-camp and special subject camp on shipping. In these activities, many China Academy of Science members, famous teachers of national level from various fields, world-renowned scientists and leading figures on the technological frontier were invited to unlock the mysteries in science and impart the methods of research.
