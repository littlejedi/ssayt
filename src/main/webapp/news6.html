第29届全国青少年科技创新大赛开幕，上海队迎接挑战

The 29th National Adolescent Science and Technology Innovation Fair is opening soon and the Shanghai team is to meet the challenge

第29届全国青少年科技创新大赛（以下简称“全国大赛”）于8月21日在北京园博园拉开帷幕，将历时一周，为全国青少年带来一场科技饕餮盛宴。上海代表队携20个参赛项目，整装待发，迎接挑战。

The 29th National Adolescent Science and Technology Innovation Fair (which is called “The national fair” in the following passage) began on August 21st in Beijing Horticultural Exposition Site and lasted for a week, bringing a big dinner of science for China’s adolescents. Shanghai was ready to meet the challenge with 20 projects.



上海代表团所有参赛项目均出自第29届上海市青少年科技创新大赛，学科涉及面丰富，包括：地球与空间、环境科学、动物学、计算机、社会科学、工程、数学和植物学等。参赛选手来自于上海多所中小学校，且都是上海青少年科学社会员，并得到了上海青少年科学社专家的细心辅导。

All the projects of the Shanghai team that took part in the contest come from the 29th Shanghai Adolescents’ Science and Technology Innovation Fair. There were lots of subjects including earth and space, environmental science, animal science, computer science, engineering, mathematics and botany. Contestants came from several middle schools and primary schools in Shanghai and all of them were members of Shanghai Science Association for Young Talents who were carefully coached by the experts of Shanghai Science Association for Young Talents.



自上海代表队21日抵京后，选手们就迅速进入备战状态，开始紧锣密鼓的赛前准备工作。本次全国大赛，除了向各位专家展示成果、封闭问辩外，参赛选手还需历经学生技能测试、学生素质测评等环节才能取得最后胜利。不过，大家都信心满满，希望能通过自己的努力获得好成绩。

Since Shanghai team arrived in Beijing on 21st, the contestants had got into the battle mood, starting the intense preparing work for the contest. During this national contest, apart from exhibiting the products to the experts and asking closed questions, contestants will take part in the students’ ability test, students’ comprehensive quality assessment before achieving success. However, everyone was filled with confidence and hope to achieve success with his or hers hard work.



全国青少年科技创新大赛是由中国科协、教育部、科技部、环境保护部、体育总局、共青团中央、全国妇联、自然科学基金委和承办省人民政府共同主办的一项全国性的青少年科技竞赛活动。大赛在培养青少年科技创新精神、实践能力和社会主义核心价值观以及提高综合素质方面发挥了重要而独特的作用，激励广大青少年努力成长为担当民族复兴的中间力量。全国青少年科技大赛曾两度在沪举办。其中，第一届大赛于1982年在上海举行。

The National Adolescent Science and Technology Innovation Fair is an adolescent science contest held together by China Association of science and technology, Ministry of education, Ministry of science and technology, Ministry of environmental protection, General Administration of sports, the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, the National Women's Federation, natural science foundation of China (NSFC) and local government. The competition plays an important role in cultivating adolescents’ spirit of scientific and technological innovation, the practical spirit, socialist core values and comprehensive qualities. It encourages the majority of young people to grow into the middle power of the national rejuvenation. The national competition was held twice in Shanghai, among which the first national contest was held in Shanghai.
