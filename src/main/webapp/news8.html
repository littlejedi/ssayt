【科学种子科普行】HOT！科学社携手美国学校共同开展自然科学的冒险！

[“Science Seed” Popular Science] HOT! SSAYT Associated with Shanghai American School (SAS) Taking an Adventure of Natural Science!



6月4日，上海青少年科学社与上海美国学校合作，带领近40名同学前往时下最热门的上海自然博物馆，遵循着自然的足迹，在“鹦鹉螺壳”内探寻地球和人类在时空演替中经历的变化。

On June the 4th, SSAYT in cooperation with SAS, led a tour to the latest and hottest Shanghai Natural History Museum with nearly 40 students. Following the footprints of nature and exploring the changes of the Earth and human beings, students have explored the changes during the succession of time inside this “nautilicone”.



“天下万物生于有，有生于无”，还有多少未解的宇宙起源之谜等待人类的探索？在历史中的生命过客和现存的生命个体又给予了人们怎样的思考？在“起源之谜”，“体验自然”，“生命长河”，“演化之道”等十余个主题场馆中，同学们走进“非洲大草原”，感受“恐龙盛世”， 参加了水陆空明星物种的“跨时空聚会”。

“All the things in this world are born from nothing.” How many mysteries about the origin of the universe are still left unsolved and waiting for humans to discover? What thoughts do the passengers in the history and the present entities provide with human beings? In the 10 theme pavilions including “the mystery of origin”, “experiencing the nature”, “endless flow of life” and “ways of evolving”, students walked into the “African grassland”, experienced the “flourishing age of dinosaurs” and took part in the “trans-time-and-space party” of the famous species from water, land and air.



参与活动的同学们带着记录本，在浏览的过程中记录下自己都所见所闻。除了生机勃勃的自然景观，自博还充满着科技元素和艺术人文气息。通过“现实增强”技术及机电模型，同学们可身临其境观察“活”的暴龙、窃蛋龙是如何生活的。而看似简单的科学绘画，则凝聚了艺术家和科学家无数次的讨论、修改，在展现艺术自由的同时，遵循严格的科学方法，把一株株植物、一只只动物，还原成栩栩如生的生命体。

Students who participated in the activity brought their notebooks and recorded what they saw. Despite the lively natural view, Shanghai Natural History Museum is full of science elements and art and humanity atmosphere. Via “reality enhancing” technology and electromechanical models, students were able to observe how “alive” tyrannosaurus and oviraptor dinosaurs lived. The scientific drawings which seem to be simple are the result of artists’ and scientists’ countless discussions and modifications. Both showing the freedom of art and following strict scientific methods, they duplicate plants and animals into lively beings.
