2015年科学教育国际论坛顺利举行

The 2015 International Science and Education Forum was held successfully



6月8日至9日，在中国青少年科技辅导员协会的组织下，上海市青少年科普促进会各区县辅协和上海市青少年科学社团体会员相关科技教育工作者赴南京参加“2015年科学教育国际论坛”。本次论坛由中国青少年科技辅导员协会主办，江苏省科协青少年科技中心、江苏省青少年科技教育协会和《中国科技教育》杂志社等单位共同承办，论坛主题为“基于探究的科学教育”。来自中国、美国、英国、加拿大的科学教育专家、学者、科技教师等共120余人参加论坛。

From June 8th to 9th, the auxiliary associations of Shanghai Youth Science Association and scientists from Shanghai Youth Science Society took part in the “2015 International Forum on Science Education” in Nanjing. This forum was hosted by China Youth Science and Technology Association of Counselors, and was organized by Association for Youth Science and Technology Center of Jiangsu Provincial, Association of Youth Science and Technology Education of Jiangsu Province and "China Science and Technology Education" magazine. The theme of this forum is “Science education is based on investigation” and more than 120 education specialists, scholars and science teachers who come from China, the US, the UK, and Canada joined the forum.



中国科协副主席、中国工程院院士、中国青少年科技辅导员协会理事长陈赛娟在致辞中强调，STEM教学需要科技教师引导学生将已知的科学知识与技术融合，将教学转变成一个探究相互联系的学习过程。并希望我国的科技教师通过本次论坛了解和学习STEM教学新理念，把握国际科学教育改革的动向和特点，探索青少年科技教育工作的新举措和新方法，推动我国科技教育向前发展。

Saijuan Chen, who is the vice president of China Association for Science, the academician of Chinese Academy of Engineering and the director of China Youth Science and Technology Association of Counselors, emphasized on her speech that STEM education requires science teachers to guide students to combine learned knowledge and technology, in order to transfer teaching into a learning process of exploring mutual relationships. And she hoped that during this forum, Chinese science teachers would not only study and comprehend the new ideas of STEM education, but they will also understand the trends and characteristics of the reform of International science education. Additionally, Saijuan Chen believed that sciences teachers will explore new methods of teenager’s science and technology education and accelerate the development of Chinese science education.



论坛共邀请21位专家、学者作了交流发言。来自美国的休伯特·迪亚斯博士和雅各布·佛斯特博士分别介绍了探究式科学教育在小学课堂中的模样及评测方式，和美国新一代科学教育标准与原有标准的变化；南京师范大学教授顾建军阐述了科学教育与技术教育融合的路径；英国的乔伊?帕文女士分享了利用产业中的具体情境来支持中小学科学教育的经验，德里克?贝尔先生揭示了探究式科学教育中高质量教学资源支持的重要性；加拿大的大卫?安德森教授分享了有效利用科技馆进行科学素质教育的策略，美国的戴耘教授结合丰富的案例作了题为《通过学生的探究和研究活动培养科学人才》的报告。论坛中，来自上海市各区县的科技辅导员和科技教育工作者也积极地参与讨论，分别向外籍专家提出了对产业情境支持科学教育等话题的看法和建议。

This forum invited 21 experts and scholars and they made several speeches. Professors Hubert Dyasi introduced exploring-based science education and its methods of testing in primary school; Professor Jacob Voss explained a new standard of American science education and its difference from the old one; Professor JianjunGu from Nanjing Normal University elaborated the ways to combine science education and technology education; Professor Joey Pavone, who comes from the UK, shared her experience that using specific circumstances to support science education; Mr. Derek Bell revealed the importance of high-quality teaching resources; Canadian Professor David Anderson introduced the strategy to use science and technology museum to promote the quality of science education and Professor Yun Dai made a report of “Bring up talented person by students’ exploring and investigation” with abundant cases. Technology instructors and technological educators from various districts in Shanghai also joined the discussion and they proposed their own views and suggestions.



在9日上午的分论坛中，上海地区参会人员分别加入到各个分论坛议题的研讨中。上海STEM云中心张逸中博士作为论坛嘉宾就“科学教育与技术教育的融合”一题发言，与会各位就论坛主题进行了深入地交流与探讨。

In the morning of June 9th, the participants from different districts of Shanghai joined the research and discussion of each sub–forum. Dr. Yizhong Zhang from Shanghai STEM Cloud Centre gave us a speech on “Fusion of science education and technology education” as a guest, and he also took part in the discussion of the forum.



同日下午，Hubert Dyasi教授和Derek Bell教授还以探究式科学教育课堂上教师的角色和形成高质量的探究式科学教育支持资源的策略为主题同与会代表进行科学教育实践的workshop研习。

On the same day’s afternoon, Professor Hubert Dyasi and Derek Bell took part in the workshop with the forum’s representations on the topic of teachers’ roles in exploring-based science class and the strategy of forming high-quality teaching resources.





此次论坛旨在推进国内外科学教育界的交流与合作，将国际最新的研究和实践进展与中国的科学教育实践相结合，为中国科学教育的实践探索提供新的视角。上海市青少年科普促进会作为上海地区STEM教育的践行者，积极地参与论坛的交流与合作中，锐意进取，开拓创新，争取为中国科学教育的实践探索做出更多更大的贡献。

This forum aims to promote the communication and cooperation of domestic and international science education circle. It also intended to combine the latest research and progress in the world with science education in China in order to provide new perspectives for the exploring of science education. Being a practitioner of STEM education in Shanghai, Shanghai Youth Science Association strive for more contributions for science education in China with innovation and enterprising.
