“2015全国骨干科技辅导员STEM课程培训班”成功举办！

2015 STEM training course for national key science instructor was successfully held!

The STEM training course for national key science instructor which was hosted by China Association of Children’s Science Instructors, East China Normal University, and held by Shanghai STEM Cloud, Shanghai Science Association for Young Talents along with Shanghai Science and Education Center was ended on 13th Dec.

The training course this time was practical, meaning it included theory explanation, decent discussion, practicals, site visit, and so on and so forth. So now let’s take a brief review of the abundant activities during that three-day training!

A fancy opening ceremony

On the December 26th, Chen Saijuan, the Chinese Academy of engineering and the president of China Association of Children’s Science Instructors, Li Xiaoliang, the vice president of China Association of Children’s Science Instructors, Lin Liqin, the secretary-general of China Association of Children’s Science Instructors, Zhu He, the deputy secretary general of China Association of Children’s Science Instructors，Yu Lizhong, the principal of NYU Shanghai campus and the president of Shanghai Adolescents Science and development committee, Ren Youqun, the vice principal of East China Normal University, and Chen Hong, the director of Shanghai Science and Education Center and the secretary general of Shanghai Adolescents Science and development committe, attended the opening ceremony.

The opening ceremony was hosted byLin Liqin, the secretary-general of China Association of Children’s Science Instructors. Followed by the welcoming speech given by Academician Chen Saijuan, Yu Lizhong, the principal of NYU Shanghai campus and the president of , Ren Youqun, the vice principal of East China Normal University, Professor Eva-Maria Neher from University of GottingenGermany, together with Dong Lanman, the director assistant of Shanghai Science and Education Center gave fantastic reports about STEM courses from different perspectives, including academy, high school and international-wise.

Creative STEM courses

In order for those science instructors to fully understand the process of STEM courses as well as the characteristics of the courses, the training have specialized STEM training courses. For example, there was the world of bridgeworkshop which required hand-on practices, Cryptology STEM, the first exploration, which introduced the method of message encryption and mathematic encryption compution, the exploration of wind energy, which required the constructing of wind energy turbines as well as data collection, what is “black”, which aimed to multiple experiments to investigate the formation of black, Amazing Fungi, which helped one to understand the gigantic living nature by studying miniscule organisms, and Decryption&chromatography, which utilized chromatography to appreciate the beauty of colors. These fascinating activities were highly praised by the instructors.

The splendid Shanghai International Popular Science Products Expo, 2015

On this Expo, the bonus materials of The three body problem, the most effective science fiction in China as well as the winner of 2015 Hugo Award, was showcased; the first solar family car from Holland, which can run for thousands of miles in one go, was displayed; The pioneering work of China, 3D printed artificial joint prosthesis also appeared on the expo. The under-training instructors visited the Expo on their last day of training and all of them gained a lot.

This training helps the instructors who participated in to reinforce the understanding of the new educational conception, STEM. Also, it enriched the instructors in terms of their scientific literacy as well as teaching skills, which will in turn help these instructors in the organization of STEM activities as well as the science and technology education of adolescents. In all, this training was crowned with complete success
