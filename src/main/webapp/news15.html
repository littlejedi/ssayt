2015年“上海市未成年人科学教育推广日——科学园丁节” 活动顺利举办！

2015 “Shanghai Science Promotion Day for Teenagers—Science Gardener Festival” was held successfully

Shanghai Science and Technology association, Shanghai Education Commission and Shanghai Science and Technology Museum held “Shanghai Science Promotion Day for Teenagers—Science Gardener Festival” (abbreviated as “Promotion Day”) at Shanghai Science Hall and some high schools. The Promotion Day was divided in to three parts: Science Gardener Festival, STEM Carnival and High School Science Open Day. The theme of the Promotion Day is “Innovation creates smart life, and the activity applied all kinds of platforms and methods to make Promotion Day perfect.

Among the three parts of the promotion day, Science Gardener Festival was held in Sep. 13th in Shanghai Science Hall. The theme of Science Gardener Festival is “Distinctive practice of STEM science education”

Shanghai Teenager Science Promotion Association’s most famous program

In 2015, Shanghai Teenager Science Promotion Association held “Shanghai Teenager Science and Technology Counselor’s essay solicitation”, “Story telling competition of young teachers “and “Put Science into practice “during the Promotion Day. After the selection, the essay solicitation activity had10 first prices, 20 second prices, 30 third prices and 3 outstanding organizations. The story telling competition and “Put Science into practice”had 17 first prices, 28 second prices and 28 third prices. In the meantime, the promotion organization also selected 5 outstanding county counselor and 7 outstanding county exemplary science promotion activities. All the award-winning works were shown on-line and off-line.

In the afternoon, activities like “Study during practice”, “STEM education in middle school” and training of new courses of STEM cloud center were held in Science Hall and awarded teacher introduced some examples. Two foreign guests took part in “STEM education in middle school” and joined the discussion.
