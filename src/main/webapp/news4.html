第十四届中国青少年机器人竞赛上海赛区选拔赛顺利举行

Auditions of 14th China Adolescent Robotics Competition Shanghai Division successfully held

2014年4月12日，由中国青少年机器人竞赛上海赛区组织委员会主办，上海科学教育中心、上海科学会堂承办，上海青少年科学社、上海市机器人学会协办的第十四届中国青少年机器人竞赛上海赛区选拔赛在上海科学会堂顺利举行。来自全市17个区县的1000多名中小学生、教练员和裁判员参加本次竞赛。

The audition of Shanghai division of 14th China Adolescent Robotics competition was successfully held in Shanghai Science Hall on April 12th, 2014. It was hosted by CARC organizing committee and was undertaken by Shanghai Education Center and Shanghai Science Hall and, meanwhile, Shanghai Science Association for Young Talents and Shanghai Robotics Society are co-organizers of the competition. Over 1000 school children, coaches and referees from 17 districts in Shanghai took part in the competition.

中国青少年机器人竞赛上海赛区选拔赛是中国青少年机器人竞赛的地区赛事，是一项将知识积累、技能培养、探究性学习融为一体，面向广大青少年机器人爱好者的科学普及性活动，旨在培养青少年的创新意识和实践能力、提高青少年的科学素质。

The CARC Shanghai division audition is a regional competition of CARC, which blends knowledge accumulation with skill development and inquiring learning. The competition is accessible to general adolescent robotics enthusiasts, aiming at cultivating innovation consciousness and practical ability of the young and improving their scientific literacy.

与往届竞赛相比，本届竞赛呈现三大特点：

The competition this year has a great improvement in three aspects:

一、 优化赛事，效率优先

I . Optimizing the schedule --- Efficiency priority

本届大赛力求赛事的系统化、高效化，本届竞赛为提升赛事整体质量，加入了各区县机器人赛事选拔，提高了参赛学生的水平，加强了赛事的严谨性。同时为贯彻党的十八大精神，精简赛程，提高效率，竞赛组委会简化本届竞赛的开幕式与闭幕式。

The competition this year seeks to be more systematic and efficient. The overall quality of the students participated and the rigorousness of the competition was strengthened by adding district auditions to the schedule. And the schedule, including opening and closing ceremonies were simplified and the efficiency was improved, so as to implement the guiding principles of the Eighteenth National Congress of the Communist Party of China.

二、 普及知识，提升素质

II. Popularizing knowledge and improving adolescents’ quality

为进一步推动机器人科学技术知识的普及,鼓励更多的青少年机器人爱好者在电子、信息、自动控制等高新科技领域进行学习、探索、研究和实践，提升参赛学生的科学素质水平，竞赛组委会办公室增设了网上素质测评环节，据统计共有6324人次参与测评。

The CARC organizing committee set up an online quality evaluation additionally this year to further help robotics knowledge gain ground and to encourage more adolescent robotics enthusiasts to learn and explore in the high and new technology of electronics, information and auto control. Statistics shows that 6324 people took part in the test.

三、 加强宣传，扩大影响

III Reinforcing propaganda and enlarging influence

利用媒体优质资源发布本届中国青少年机器人竞赛上海赛区选拔赛赛事信息，通过上海青少年科技英才网、上海青少年科学社微信等公众平台及时报道竞赛进展及相关新闻，扩大了科普受众面和竞赛影响力。据统计共有26000余人次通过网站及微信平台关注本届竞赛信息。

The competition successfully enlarged its audience and influence bytaking full advantage of media such as www.shssp.org and SSAYT WeChat official account platform to report the schedule and competition-related news without delay. According to statistics, over 26000 visitors gave much attention to CARC through the website and WeChat platform.
