“创新·体验·成长” 第29届上海市青少年科技创新大赛圆满闭幕

“Innovation, Experience, Growth”, the 29th Shanghai Adolescents’ Science and Technology Innovation Contest Successfully Concluded.

2014年3月23日，第29届上海市青少年科技创新大赛在上海科学会堂圆满闭幕！本届大赛共评选出青少年科技创新成果一等奖315项、专项奖551项，科技辅导员科教创新成果一等奖10个，优秀科技辅导员12名，少年儿童科学幻想画一等奖64幅，青少年科技实践活动一等奖20名；评选出24个优秀青少年科技创新项目，推荐参加第29届全国青少年科技创新大赛。

The 29th Shanghai Adolescents’ Science and Technology Innovation Fair Successfully Concluded in Shanghai Science Hall on March 23rd, 2014. A total of 315 first prizes in Adolescent Science and Technology Innovation Awards, 551 special awards, 10 first prizes in ScienceCounselors Educational Innovation Awards, 12 outstanding Science Counselors, 64 first prizes in Children’s Science Fiction Painting, 20 first prizes in Adolescent Scientific Activities are elected during this session. 24 outstanding Adolescent Science and Technology Innovation projects are selected and recommended for the 29th National Adolescent Science and Technology Innovation Fair.

黄浦区副区长吴成颁发特别贡献奖
Huangpu District’s vice mayor Wu Cheng awarded Special Contribution Award


本届大赛共有17个区县的30多万师生积极参与。经区县初赛，共收到2753份申报材料，其中青少年科技创新成果2080件、科技辅导员科教创新成果44件、少年儿童科学幻想画451幅、青少年科技实践活动118项、科技辅导员科技教育方案60个。经专家网上初评，最终有699项青少年科技创新成果、46项科技辅导员科技教育方案入围终评，评出15项科技辅导员科教创新成果、63项青少年科技实践活动和64幅少年儿童科学幻想绘画参与展示。同时，本届大赛充分利用了微信平台，让更多的人了解并参与了这场展示青少年科技创新成果的盛会。

Over 300 thousand teachers and students from 17 districts took part in the competition. After the district preliminary contest, we received 2753 applications, including 2080 adolescent science and technology innovation products, 44 science counselor’s innovation products, 451 children’s science fiction paintings, 118 adolescent scientific activities, 60 science counselors’ educational plans. After specialists’ online preliminary assessment, 699 adolescent science and technology innovation products, 46 science counselor’s innovation products were chosen for the finals, while 15 science counselor’s innovation products, 63 adolescent scientific activities and science counselors’ educational plans, 64 children’s science fiction paintings were chosen for the exhibition. Meanwhile, the competition made full use of wechat platform, enabling more people to know and take part in the grand gathering of adolescent science and technology innovation products.
