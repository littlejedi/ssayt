2015年全国中学生生物学联赛（上海赛区）顺利举行

2015 National Middle School Students Biology League Contest (Shanghai District) Held Successfully

2015年5月10日上午，由上海市植物学会和上海市动物学会共同主办的2015年全国中学生生物学联赛（上海赛区）在上海科学会堂顺利举行。本次竞赛共吸引了964名高中生报名参赛，根据竞赛成绩评选出一二三等奖外，还将选出8名选手代表上海地区参加2015年全国中学生生物学联赛。

On the morning of May 10, 2015, 2015 National High School Biology league (Shanghai) which sponsored by the Shanghai Institute of Botany and Shanghai Institute of zoology in Shanghai Science Hall was held successfully. About 964 high school students had enrolled in the competition. According to the results of the competition,not only the first,second and the third prize will be awarded, but also will select 8 players to represent Shanghai region to participate in the National Middle School Biology league in 2015.



本次竞赛呈现三大特色：

1、 回归科学殿堂，感受科学魅力

与往届不同的是，本届竞赛主考场设在上海科学会堂，分考场设在上海市向明中学。在紧张的竞赛之余，让学生走进科学的殿堂，感受最前沿的科学魅力。

What was different from the previous was that, the main contest hall established in Shanghai science hall, while Shanghai Xiang Ming middle school was the sub filed. The students can reach to the scientific palace in the tension of the competition, feeling the most advanced scientific charm.

2、 统一线上报名，STEM专项测评

学生通过线上报名系统报名，并在“STEM综合素质测评系统”参加在线生物学科专项STEM测评。 “STEM综合素质测评系统”拥有市科协下属60余家专业学会精心打造了10000余道题目，范围涉及生命科学、物质科学、技术与设计、地球与空间、社会与行为学5大领域16个子学科。本次专项测评在主办学会建议下选取了动物、植物、细胞、遗传、生态环境等相关学科试题，测评结果为主办学会评价學生提供參考。

Students registered through the online registration system, and to participate in the online biological specific STEM evaluation in the "STEM comprehensive quality evaluation system" ."STEM" with more than 60 professional society which belongs to Shanghai Association For Science And Technology had meticulously created more than 10000 items, ranging from 5 major areas 16 sub areas of life science, physical science, technology and design, earth and space, social and behavioral science. The evaluation selected animal, plant, cell, genetic, ecological environment and other related subjects according to the host society’s proposal. The evaluation results provide reference for the host society to evaluate students.

3、 规范组织管理，打造品牌赛事

本届竞赛，上海市中学生五项学科竞赛管理委员会办公室按照中国科协的相关管理条例全程监督，确保了赛事规范运作。

Shanghai five-discipline-competition for middle school students Management Office Commission supervised the whole competition this year, according to the relevant regulations of China Association For Science and Technology, which ensured that the tournament operated canonically.

另外，竞赛信息将通过上海青少年科学社官方微信发布，不仅能在赛前扩大了宣传面，提升了宣传力度，还能让参赛学生及时准确获取赛事信息，查看比赛成绩。本次2015年全国中学生生物学联赛（上海赛区）的成绩将于近期在上海青少年科学社官方微信公布，敬请期待。

In addition, the competition information will be issued by the Shanghai Science Association for Young Talents’ official Wechat account. This motion will not only to expand the publicity front, enhance the publicity efforts, but also allows students to get the event information in a timely manner to acquire the results of the competition. The results of the 2015 National High School Biology league (Shanghai) will be published on the Shanghai Science Association for Young Talents’ official Wechat account soon.



关于全国中学生五项学科竞赛

全国中学生五项学科竞赛是面向全国中学生开展的课外科学竞赛活动。宗旨是向中学生普及科学知识，激发中学生学习科学的兴趣和积极性，为中学生提供互相交流和学习的机会；促进中等学校教学改革；通过竞赛和相关活动培养和选拔优秀学生，为参加国际学科竞赛选拔参赛选手。

National Middle School Students' five subject competition is a science competition for middle school students. It aims to popularize scientific knowledge to students, to stimulate students' interest and enthusiasm in learning science. It also provide students with opportunities to communicate and learn, to promote the teaching reform of secondary schools, and to train and select outstanding students through competitions and related activities for taking part in the international competition.

根据《中国科协办公厅关于印发<全国中学生五项学科竞赛管理条例（修订）>的通知》（科协办发青字[2014]40号）精神，为进一步完善上海市中学生五项学科竞赛组织管理工作，经上海市科协党组2015年第一次会议审议并通过，决定成立上海市中学生五项学科竞赛管理委员会，主要负责监督、协调本市学科竞赛的各项组织工作；监督本赛区竞赛（联赛）组织实施单位的工作；审核本赛区竞赛获奖学生名单。上海市中学生五项学科竞赛管理委员会下设管理委员会办公室，主要负责处理日常工作。办公室设在上海市科学技术协会上海科学教育中心。

According to the spirit of < China Association for Science and Technology Office's notice about  the issuance of < National five-discipline-competition for middle school students Regulations  (Amendment)  "notice" > (AST co-blue word [2014] No. 40) > , to further improve the  management of Shanghai five-discipline competition for middle school students organization,  Shanghai Association for Science and Technology party deliberate and approve the decision to  set up Shanghai five-discipline competition  management committee in 2015, during the first conference. This committee is chiefly responsible for supervising and coordinating the organization of the city's  academic competition and implementing of the Division contest (League) organization;  auditing the list of division race winners.  Shanghai five-discipline-competition for middle school students management committee has Regulatory Commission  Office which is primarily responsible for routine work,  locating in Shanghai Science and Technology Education Center under Shanghai Association for  Science and Technology.
